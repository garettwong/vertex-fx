A browser canvas simulates liquid dye plumes. Page scroll scrubs progress like a video timeline, suitable for beauty, wellness, product launches and campaign sites.

Technique

Progress maps to colour, density, and copy.

The section does not play a video. It redraws procedural ink fields using scroll progress, mouse pressure and time.
