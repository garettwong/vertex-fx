01 / follow

Lag creates personality

The fish aims at the pointer but eases toward it, so movement has mass and direction instead of a flat SVG stuck to the mouse.

02 / trace

Rings prove responsiveness

Canvas ripples are generated from pointer velocity and touch taps. They fade quickly to avoid muddying typography.

Mobile is not ignored

On touch devices the companion idles in a loop and jumps to taps; no hidden desktop-only cursor dependency.

Use this pattern for playful brands, onboarding pages, kids products, game launches, or any hero where the cursor can become a brand mascot.
