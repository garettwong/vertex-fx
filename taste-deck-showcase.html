Not a particle effect. The reusable FX is a polished product-marketing composition: mockups orbiting a command card, strict design controls and documentation panels that feel alive while staying readable.

Overlapping UI cards drift in 3D so the landing page feels premium without relying on copied screenshots or brand assets.
