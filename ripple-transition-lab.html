Wave choreography

A click launches an expanding ring from the pointer. Pixels near the ring sample a displaced source/target image, creating the watery refraction.

Premium detail

Chromatic edge glow, noise wobble, pinch pull and fading caustic lines make a normal image swap feel like a real component.

Asset safety

The landscapes are generated with gradients, mountains, tree strokes and noise. No Componentry code, video frames, photos or docs content are copied.
