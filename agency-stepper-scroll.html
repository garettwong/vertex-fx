Reusable pattern

Make an agency site feel directed, not decorated.

The important behavior is simple: each content section owns a visual state. As the visitor scrolls, the hero preview, number, palette and project rail update together.

Why this belongs in the FX library.

Technique

Intersection-driven state

IntersectionObserver maps scroll chapters to one reusable stage component: number, copy, palette, progress and cards all change together.

Client use

Agency and portfolio ready

The pattern works for creative studios, consultants, SaaS showcases and any landing page that needs structure plus premium motion.

Safety

Concept-only rebuild

Inspired by a high-quality X post, rebuilt from scratch with fictional brand, generated UI panels and no copied assets, screenshots, videos, code or logos.
